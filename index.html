// HOW TO CONNECT YOUR GOOGLE SHEET
Two tabs, two URLs. Paste each one in turn — the dashboard auto-detects which tab is which by looking at column headers.
1. Make the Sheet public (one-time):
  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Click Share (top right)
  3. Under "General access", change to Anyone with the link → Viewer
  4. Click Done
2. Paste the URL of each tab:
  1. Click on the NOC tab in your Sheet
  2. Copy the URL from your browser (any form works — edit, share, pub)
  3. Paste here and click Load Sheet
  4. Repeat for the HoReCa Onboarding tab
Tab 1 — NOC. Required columns: Panchayat, Block, NOC. Optional: Target. NOC values: Received / Pending / Yes / No.
Tab 2 — HoReCa Onboarding. Required columns: business_name, status. Status values: ACTIVE = onboarded, DRAFT = in pipeline.
Once loaded, both URLs are saved. On next page load both tabs auto-refresh from the Sheet — no re-pasting needed.
